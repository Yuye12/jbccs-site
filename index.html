Our Heritage

JBCCS (James Bay Cree Cultural Education Centre) is more than just a radio station. We are the heartbeat of Mistissini, bridging the gap between our elders' wisdom and our youth's future. For decades, we have been dedicated to the preservation of Cree language and the enrichment of our community's cultural life.
